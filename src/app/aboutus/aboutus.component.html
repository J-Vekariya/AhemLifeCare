Pharmaceutical
Pharmaceutical industry discovers, develops, produces, and markets drugs or pharmaceuticals for use as medications. Pharmaceutical companies may deal in generic or brand medications and medical devices. They are subject to a variety of laws and regulations that govern the patenting, testing, safety, efficacy and marketing of drugs

Nutraceutical
Nutraceutical, the term is applied to products that range from isolated nutrients, dietary supplements and herbal products, specific diets and processed foods.

Ayurveda
Ayurveda medicine, is a system of medicine with historical roots in the Indian subcontinent. Globalized and modernized practices derived from Ayurveda traditions are a type of complementary or alternative medicine.
